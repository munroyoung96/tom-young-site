I'm Tom Young — based in east village. Work in williamsburg. Play soccer in manhattan. Generate steady volume of haha's in group chats.

I went to college at Williams, where I majored in economics and logged another chapter of my soccer career. I captained the team but also suffered a really gnarly leg injury that cut my story short.

My final internship before graduating was at the Williams College Investment Office, but I realized that Boston and traditional finance was not the path I wanted to take out of the post-grad gates.

I moved back to NY after graduating and have stitched together a few career moves since — sales and strategy at CB Insights, finance at Sorare, and now strategic finance at Whop. Each step was a deeper pull into a thing I didn't know I wanted to do until I was already doing it.

I haven't let soccer go completely, serving on a team called Pete's Dragons since 2019. I'm a frequent runner and ran the 2024 NYC Marathon.

I value my close circles pretty fiercely. I find divinity in a coffee chat with a close friend in the east village. I hold sacred a long weekend in Davis Park with my family.
